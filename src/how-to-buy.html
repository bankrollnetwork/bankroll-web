How to Buy VLT

A simple path from wallet setup to a verified Uniswap swap for Bankroll Vault.

Download Base app Buy on Uniswap Download PDF

Start here

Get your wallet ready before you swap.

VLT lives on Ethereum and trades through Uniswap V2. You do not need an account with Bankroll to hold it. You need a self-custody wallet, ETH for the swap and gas, and the correct VLT contract address.

01

Install a wallet

Use the Base App to self-custody and fund your wallet. Keep your recovery phrase private. No Bankroll page will ever need it.

02

Fund with ETH

VLT is bought through Ethereum liquidity. Make sure your wallet has enough ETH for the swap and network gas.

03

Open Uniswap

Use the VLT buy link from this site or paste the verified contract address into Uniswap yourself.

04

Verify the asset

Confirm the token is Bankroll Vault and the contract matches the address shown below before approving anything.

05

Review the swap

Check output amount, price impact, slippage, gas, and the wallet confirmation screen. Estimates can move as the pool changes.

06

Hold and verify

After the transaction confirms, your VLT balance should appear in your wallet. You can also verify the transaction on Etherscan.
