What Bankroll Vault is

A decentralized digital asset with a rising floor price.

VLT is the store-of-value token of the Bankroll Network, deployed on Ethereum in 2020. It pioneered the Proof of Liquidity model: verified liquidity that cannot be pulled by any central authority.

The 1.8M fixed supply trades against a Uniswap V2 pool that is perpetually supported by the broader Bankroll ecosystem. Trading fees accrue directly into the pool, deepening intrinsic value as volume and adoption rise.

Fully decentralized. No admin keys. No mint function. No team unlocks. Every contract is immutable and accessible directly onchain.
