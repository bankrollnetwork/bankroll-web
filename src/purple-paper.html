Core idea

Liquidity is the proof.

VLT is designed so the story can be checked directly from Ethereum. The contract exists, the supply is fixed, the Uniswap V2 market is live, and the liquidity model is visible onchain.

Fixed supply

The maximum supply is 1,800,000 VLT. There is no mint function and no hidden future issuance path. Scarcity is simple enough for anyone to count.

Secured liquidity

VLT trades against a Uniswap V2 pool that the broader Bankroll ecosystem can support over time. Liquidity, volume, swaps, and price can be inspected through public Ethereum data.

Proof of Liquidity

Proof of Liquidity means the market base matters. A digital asset should not depend only on a slogan or a chart. It should have visible liquidity that users can verify before participating.

Value capture

Every swap pays fees into the pool. As activity grows, the market can deepen, the base can strengthen, and the asset can become more interesting to long-term participants.

Structure is live. Awareness is early.

The honest pitch is not a guaranteed outcome. The honest pitch is fixed supply, live liquidity, public contracts, and early awareness.
