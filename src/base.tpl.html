FAQ

Bankroll is a permissionless system for economic inclusion and helps its community increase the velocity of their income

Balance Protection

The corner stone of Bankroll is a simple mechanism that dynamically caps daily withdrawals at 0.05 - 0.025% of the total balance in contract. Most users will never be affected by the debt ceiling. This allows the contract to grow in a distributed and decentralized fashion

 

Lifetime Dividends

Players enjoy 100% of all deposits into the dividend pool, paid out daily at 3.33%. All commission fees are credited to account holders and paid out for the lifetime of the contract. 1.25% each for referral / custody

 

Longevity and Sustainability

The contract is designed to be a reusable solution for profit sharing. It is designed to be used by other contracts so funds are flowing in from a large TRON ecosystem. Developers can leverage both donations and sponsorships to credit their users or incentivize users of Bankroll to participate.

 

Withdraw Anytime

Withdraw your dividends anytime on a per minute basis. No need to wait 24 hours.









 

Sleek and Responsive

The system is designed to work well on both desktop and mobile. A sleek dark theme keeps your information confidential and private
