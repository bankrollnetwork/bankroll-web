About

VLT is the store-of-value token of the Bankroll Network, deployed on Ethereum in 2020. It pioneered the Proof of Liquidity (POL) model: verified liquidity that cannot be pulled by any central authority.

The 1.8M fixed supply trades against a Uniswap V2 pool that is perpetually supported by the broader Bankroll ecosystem. Trading fees accrue directly into the pool, deepening intrinsic value as volume and adoption rise — the longer VLT trades, the thicker the floor gets.

Fully decentralized. No admin keys. No mint function. No team unlocks. Every contract is immutable and accessible directly on-chain.

Key facts

  • Max supply: 1,800,000 VLT (fixed)
  • Chain: Ethereum (ERC-20)
  • Contract: 0x6b785a0322126826d8226d77e173d75DAfb84d11
  • Primary liquidity: Uniswap V2
  • Ecosystem: Bankroll Network
